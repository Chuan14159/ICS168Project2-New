Game: DodgeBall2D
Team: TEKC
==================================================================
Description: DodgeBall2D is a teambase competitive game that is all fun and skill. Each team will be placed against each other in a match of whose team will have more strike point than other.
==================================================================
Controls:
AWSD - to move.
F - to pick up.
LMB - to throw.
Space - to jump; 2X Space to double jump.

Mechanics:
	Move cursor will aim at the direction you want to throw.
	Double jump is a fun mechanic to dodge incoming projectile.
